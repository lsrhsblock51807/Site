Why Being a Good Citizen Matters

Where I got this photo

Good citizenship is not just about doing things here and there, it also requires making daily decisions that don't just benefit you. A good citizen always looks out for those who cannot always look out for themselves. Being honest, compassionate and courageous are among the chief characteristics of a model citizen.

Each characteristic of a good citizen is equally important as the other. For example, one would not have the motivation to help someone without compassion and an understanding of their situation.

Here are some things that you can do every day that can make you a better citizen

  • Helping someone with ther groceries
  • holding the door for others
  • Helping someone when they're hurt
  • Just being a nice person day in and day out

I myself performed such an act of citizenahip by helping someone in the store who had dropped all of her items on the store. Instead of just walking by like a normal pedestrian, I wanted to take the initiative to step up and be better. After, I couldn't help but feel quite staisfied with what i did. Which further affirmed the notion that citizenship actually makes you feel better.
